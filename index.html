About the School

South Wiru High School is located in South Wiru LLG, Ialibu-Pangia District, Southern Highlands Province. It is one of the fastest developing high schools in the region.
Situated in the Pangia area, the school features modern classrooms, staff housing, and continued infrastructure growth.

Leadership & Funding

Supported by the Southern Highlands Provincial Government under Governor William Powi and South Wiru LLG President Joseph Kunukunu.
The school has received approximately K4 million in development funding to strengthen infrastructure and academic excellence.

Milestones

The school celebrated its inaugural Grade 10 graduation on December 6, 2024 — marking a historic milestone.
